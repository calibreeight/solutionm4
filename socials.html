Frequently Asked Questions

These are some of our most common questions and answers.

What is social media advertising?

Social media advertising is a form of online advertising that allows you to reach a specific audience based on their interests and personal information. Social media advertising can be done through the use of paid ads on social media websites such as Facebook, Twitter, YouTube and others.

Can I do social media advertising myself?

You can, but we wouldn't recommend it. Social media advertising is a complex process that requires knowledge of how different platforms work and how to optimize your ads for maximum effect. This can take a lot of time, effort and money to do properly, which is why many businesses turn to social media advertising agencies.

How long do social media ads take to see results?

The answer to this question depends on many factors, including your target audience and what type of campaign you're running. However, we generally start seeing results within the first 30 days for most social media advertising campaigns.

How much are your social media advertising services?

The price of our social media advertising services are determined by what is required for your campaign and your total ad budget. Get in touch with our ads team today for a custom quote.

Why is social media advertising worth the investment?

Social media advertising is the best way to reach your target audience because it's highly targeted. As a result, you're able to reach people more efficiently with fewer dollars than other marketing channels, including TV and radio ads.

How do I know that Facebook is the right ads platform for my business?

Facebook is one of the best social media platforms for advertisers because it offers a variety of ad types that you can use to reach your target audience. It's also a great place to start if you're new to paid advertising since it can help find your target audience faster than most other advertising platforms.
