Frequently Asked Questions

These are some of our most common questions and answers.

What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) is a way of improving the visibility of a website in search engines like Google, Bing and Yahoo! It's a process that helps you get your site ranked higher in search rankings for particular keywords.

It involves improving the way your website appears in search results, making it more likely to attract visitors and make them want to click through to your site.

Can I do SEO myself?

You can do SEO by yourself, but it's important to understand that SEO isn't something you can learn overnight. It takes time, research, and dedication to get good at it. Plus a lot of trial and error, which can take months if not years. So if you're looking for someone who'll handle the technical stuff while you focus on your business, we're here for you!

How long does SEO take to see results?

SEO takes time to see results. We start to see results after 3 months, but the best results are often between 6-12 months.

How much are your SEO services?

We tailor our SEO services to fit your business, niche and goals. If you would like a custom quote, feel free to get in touch with our team today.

Why is SEO important?

SEO is important for your business because it can help you reach more customers and grow your business.

Search engines like Google are the primary way that people find companies online, and SEO is a set of techniques that help your business appear in search results.

The more people who see your website in search results, the more customers you'll have.

How many keywords do you target?

We don't have a set number of keywords or search volume. We put a lot of time into researching your industry and finding the most valuable keywords that will bring you a positive return. Rather than targeting 50 keywords that might get your business 1 sale a month, we'd rather target 5 keywords that results in 50 sales a month.
