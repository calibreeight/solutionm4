Frequently Asked Questions

These are some of our most common questions and answers.

What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing is a way to reach your target audience online through the use of social media platforms, search engines, email and other digital marketing channels. It's a cost-effective way to reach customers because it's highly targeted. As a result, you're able to reach people more efficiently with fewer dollars than other traditional marketing channels, such as TV and radio ads.

How much do your services cost?

We tailor our digital marketing services to fit your business, niche and goals. If you would like a custom quote, feel free to get in touch with our team today.

How do I get started?

Simply give us a call or fill out the form below, and one of our digital marketing experts will get in touch and guide you through the process.

Do you offer once-off marketing campaigns?

We don't usually offer once-off marketing campaigns, as a large part of our process relies on gathering data and further improving your campaign. We prefer long-term partnerships with businesses who are interested in growing their business.

Is SEO or PPC better for my business?

It's hard to say without jumping on a call first. This depends on a number of factors, including budget, product/service, competition, website quality, and many more. Talk to our team to help figure out which is the best option for you.

How long does it take to get results?

This depends on the service. Social media advertising and Google Ads can see results in the first week, but SEO is often a long-term strategy that provides a higher return on investment over time.
