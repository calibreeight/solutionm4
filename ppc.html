Frequently Asked Questions

These are some of our most common questions and answers.

What is PPC?

Pay per click (PPC) is a form of internet advertising that allows businesses to pay for their ads only when users click on them. The goal is to attract new customers and increase sales by putting your ads in front of people who are actively looking for what you offer.

Can I do Google Ads myself?

You can do Google Ads yourself, but it’s not the best idea. You don’t have to be an expert in order to create a Google Ads campaign, but there are plenty of things that can go wrong if you don’t know what you’re doing. Newcomers often waste a lot of money while trying to learn the ropes, and may never get a positive return. This is why we recommend working with a digital marketing agency, as their staff have already overcome the learning curve and have a proven track record of success.

How long does PPC take to see results?

Our PPC services often see results in the first month, but the results should continue to improve over time. The more data that your campaign collects, the better our specialists can optimise your campaign towards your ideal customer.

How much are your PPC services?

The price of our PPC services are determined by the type of campaign and your total ad budget. Get in touch with our ads team today for a custom quote.

Why is PPC important?

PPC is one of the most effective digital marketing tools for local businesses. It allows you to get your brand in front of people who are actively searching for what you offer, so they can decide whether or not to buy from you. This kind of targeted advertising makes your campaign more cost-effective than other strategies, like social media ads and SEO.

How many keywords do you target?

We don't have a set number of keywords to target. Our specialists can target as many keywords as you want, but will always prioritise getting you a positive return. This means that if your keywords aren't converting, they will be cut out of the campaign.
